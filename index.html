Our mission is to provide affordable, reliable transportation options, foster a healthy and sustainable environment, and build a sense of community through cycling.

Community Cycles Seguin is a community-based non-profit organization that promotes cycling through volunteer work to repair and make ready donated and public bicycles.

Get Involved

If you are passionate about cycling and want to contribute to our cause, consider joining our volunteer programs. Help us repair and maintain bicycles to make them accessible to everyone in the community.
